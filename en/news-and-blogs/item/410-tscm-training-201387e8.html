TSCM Training 2013

  • Read 2926 times
TSCM seminar will take place on 19-20 of February in Klaipeda, Lithuania. Seminar duration 2 days. Admission will be free. Developers from leading manufacturing companies will present their new equipment and be ready to answer any questions. Attendees will receive participation certificates.
The following products will be presented and shown in operation:
1. a wholly new ST-031M multifunction counter-surveillance device
2. a new generation nonlinear junction detector of Russian origin
3. Lornet series NLJD (with technical overview and practical workshop)
4. ARCAM - detector of wired video cameras

Some previous experience in TSCM and basic understanding of electronics are desirable but not mandatory.

Coffee and snacks will be served during breaks.
Suggested airports for arrival: Palanga (PLQ) 25 km from Klaipeda. We will arrange pickup from Palanga airport.
