Results of Securex 2014

  • Read 2655 times

On 08-11 of April 2014 JSC Selcom Security successfully attended International Security Fair SECUREX 2014, in Poznan, Poland. The exhibition is being held every two years and is one of the largest exhibitions in Poland. Over twenty four thousand people attended this exhibition. Visitors showed great interest towards these devices. We would like to say thank you to the vizitors and to exhibition organizers for positive attitude and high level of the current event!
