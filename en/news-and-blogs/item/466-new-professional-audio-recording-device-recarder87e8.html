New Professional Audio recording device "reCARDer"

  • Read 3064 times

SELCOM SECURITY JSC started serial production of professional audio recording device - reCARDer , in a form of standart plastic card. This family of digital audio recorders is designed for professional audio recording. Devices are produced in a plastic card body, 1.6 mm thick (identical to SMART and HID-cards). The device records audio data in a special format files which can’t be played on other recorders and computer.

If you are interested in these products, please do not hesitate to contact us with any question.
