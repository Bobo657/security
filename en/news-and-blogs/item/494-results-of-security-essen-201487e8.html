Results of Security Essen 2014

  • Read 2877 times
On 23-26 of September 2014 JSC Selcom Security successfully attended International Security Fair Security Essen 2014, in Essen, Germany. The exhibition is being held every two years and is one of the largest security exhibitions in Europe. Over forty thousand trade visitors from 110 countries attended this exhibition. Visitors showed great interest towards our TSCM devices. We would like to say thank you to visitors and to exhibition organizers for positive attitude, support and high level of the current event!
