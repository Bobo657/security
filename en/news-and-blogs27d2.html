Results of Milipol 2013

19-22 of November 2013 JSC Selcom Security successfully attended MILIPOL 2013 exhibition, in Paris. The exhibition is being held every two years and is one of the largest exhibitions in Europe in the area of newest means and services in security sphere. The total exhibition area - 20 thousand square meters. In 2013 about 900 companies from 37 countries took part in the exhibition.

In this event our company along with the manufacturer of the equipment company Suritel had announced following new items:

*Hidden video camera detector SEL SP-102 ARCAM, the device that has no analogues in the world;

*Non-linear junction detector ST-400 Cayman, fundamentally new non-linear junction detector with high technical characteristics.

Exhibition visitors showed great interest towards these devices, understanding of uniqueness of technical opportunities.

We were delighted that big manufacturers and users of TSCM equipment showed interest towards our booth.

We would like to thank the organizers for positive attitude and high level of the current event!
